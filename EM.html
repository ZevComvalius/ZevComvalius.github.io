Electric motor

With a 3D printer, some wire and two magnets a friend and me built a functioning electric motor. We applied our knowledge of physics and came up with a design we could build. If you connect the two wires on the collector to a voltage the motor starts spinning surprisingly fast. After building a prototype which sadly did not work, we reflected and built a second version which did work. We then measured various specifications of the motor.
